This Privacy Policy describes how CNS Studios ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use this website and the file-sharing service ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • User Content: When you drop a file, we upload the encrypted content you submit and the expiration duration you select to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide the Service, specifically to store and retrieve your files for the duration you specify.

3. Data Retention

Files and uploaded data is automatically deleted from our servers after the expiration time you selected (e.g., 1 hour, 24 hours).
