Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CNS Studios ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use this website and the temporary note-sharing service ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • User Content: When you create a note, we store the text content you submit and the expiration duration you select.
  • Usage Data: We may collect standard server logs, including IP addresses and request timestamps, to monitor system health and prevent abuse. IP addresses are specifically monitored to enforce rate limits on file reporting and to prevent spam.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide the Service, specifically to store and retrieve your notes for the duration you specify.

3. Data Retention

Notes and uploaded data is automatically deleted from our servers after the expiration time you selected (e.g., 1 hour, 24 hours). Notes set to "infinite" are retained until manually deleted or until the service is discontinued.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@cns-studios.com.
