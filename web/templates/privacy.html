Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

SecureShare ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our file sharing service.

2. End-to-End Encryption

SecureShare uses client-side end-to-end encryption. This means:

  • Your files are encrypted in your browser before upload
  • Encryption keys are generated on your device
  • We never receive or store your encryption keys
  • We cannot decrypt or access the contents of your files
  • Only people with the password can decrypt the files

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Encrypted file data: We store the encrypted blob of your file, which we cannot decrypt
  • File metadata: Original filename, file size, upload time, expiration time

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: We collect the IP address of uploaders and reporters for abuse prevention
  • Timestamps: When files are uploaded, accessed, or reported

3.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Encryption keys or passwords
  • Decrypted file contents
  • Personal identification beyond IP addresses
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Browser fingerprints

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the file sharing service
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Prevent abuse and illegal activity
  • Respond to legal requests when required
  • Automatically delete files after expiration

5. Data Retention

Encrypted files: Automatically deleted after the selected expiration period (24 hours to 3 months).

Metadata and logs: May be retained for up to 30 days after file deletion for abuse prevention purposes.

Reported files: Files with multiple reports may be deleted immediately.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information only:

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To prevent illegal activity

Note: Even if compelled, we cannot provide decrypted file contents because we do not possess the encryption keys.

7. Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption (client-side)
  • PBKDF2 key derivation with 100,000 iterations
  • Secure random generation for passwords
  • HTTPS for all connections
  • Regular security updates

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Not use the service if you disagree with this policy
  • Choose short expiration periods for your files
  • Report files that violate our terms

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for infrastructure (hosting, CDN). These services may have access to encrypted data in transit but cannot decrypt it.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the appropriate channels.
